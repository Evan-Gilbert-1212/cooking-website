My Cooking Journey!

More great cooking pictures!

My passion for cooking just started recently. For years I didn't care what I ate and just ate because I had to. Now, I have realized that I wanted to get more into cooking and making things that have more variety and that are healthier for me. I definitely want to be able to get better and learn how to make a wide variety of meals.

I want to find dishes that are good, but also have plenty of protein and vegetables to make them balanced. So, my focus has been on finding recipes that have a good balance of everything. Not every meal will be perfect, but the more I think about what goes into the meal, the better off my cooking will be! So far, I have cooked the following dishes:

  • Chicken Curry
  • Green Chile Stew
  • Chicken Enchiladas

I look forward to expanding this list and getting better at cooking all my favorite meals!
